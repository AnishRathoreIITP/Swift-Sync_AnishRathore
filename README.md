# 🌱 Swift Sync

### One Plate Saved. One Life Changed.

Swift Sync is a real-time resource redistribution platform that connects surplus food and essential resources with NGOs and communities — reducing waste and fighting hunger efficiently.

---

## Project Vision

Every day, tons of food and useful resources are wasted while millions of people go hungry.

**Swift Sync aims to bridge this gap.**

Our vision is simple:

> *No food should be wasted while people are starving.*

---

## Project Motto

**"Zero Waste · Zero Hunger"**

We believe that even one small action — like saving one plate of food — can change someone’s life.

---

##  Problem Statement

* Massive food wastage at events, hotels, and homes
* Lack of real-time connection between donors and NGOs
* Delays in distribution causing food spoilage
* Millions of people still sleep hungry

---

## Solution

Swift Sync provides a **real-time platform** where:

* Donors can instantly post surplus food/resources
* NGOs receive alerts immediately
* Resources are matched within the same city
* Urgent needs are fulfilled quickly
